Frequently Asked Questions

Is cryptocurrency legal?

The legal status of cryptocurrency varies by jurisdiction. While most countries allow cryptocurrency trading and use, regulations differ significantly. We can help you understand the specific legal framework in your target markets.

What are the regulatory requirements for launching an ICO/STO?

Regulatory requirements depend on the jurisdiction and the nature of the token being offered. Most jurisdictions require compliance with securities laws, anti-money laundering regulations, and consumer protection laws.

How are cryptocurrencies taxed?

Tax treatment of cryptocurrencies varies by country. Many jurisdictions treat cryptocurrency as property for tax purposes, meaning capital gains taxes may apply to transactions. We can help you understand your tax obligations.

What are the risks of operating a cryptocurrency exchange?

Cryptocurrency exchanges face regulatory, security, and operational risks. These include compliance with AML/KYC regulations, cybersecurity threats, and the need for proper licensing in many jurisdictions.

How can I ensure my smart contract is legally enforceable?

To ensure enforceability, smart contracts should be drafted with clear terms, comply with applicable laws, and include dispute resolution mechanisms. Our team can review and help draft legally sound smart contracts.
