Frequently Asked Questions

What makes fintech and crypto bankruptcies different from traditional bankruptcies?

Fintech and crypto bankruptcies often involve unique challenges such as digital asset valuation, cross-border jurisdictional issues, and novel legal questions surrounding blockchain technology and smart contracts.

How can I protect my interests in a fintech or crypto bankruptcy?

Early intervention is crucial. Contacting experienced counsel as soon as possible can help protect your rights and maximize potential recoveries.

What should I do if I suspect fraud in a fintech or crypto company?

Document all transactions and communications, and consult with legal counsel immediately to explore your options for asset recovery and potential legal action.

How are digital assets treated in bankruptcy proceedings?

The treatment of digital assets in bankruptcy is an evolving area of law. Courts are still establishing precedents, making experienced legal counsel essential for navigating these complex cases.
