Disputes.
That’s All We Do.

The Great Recovery, with offices in Boston, New York, and Miami, helps our clients solve problems.

We are trusted advisors, and we provide zealous, aggressive, and sophisticated advocacy when our clients need it the most. We are trial ready. We achieve precedent-setting results in and out of the courtroom.

On the civil side, our clients are corporations, partnerships, LLCs, individuals, or collective consumers embroiled in complicated high-stakes disputes typically involving a minimum of $10 million—and up to billions—in damages.

Our clients too face investigation or charges from regulatory agencies such as the Department of Justice, the Securities and Exchange Commission, the CFTC, and various other federal, state and local agencies. If the dispute requires aggressive, strategic litigation, clients retain Dynamis.

We’re not “plaintiffs’” or “defense” attorneys. We’re just on the winning side of the “v,” because we’ll strategically and tirelessly pursue that result for each client.

White-Collar Criminal and Regulatory Defense

Led by renowned former federal prosecutor Eric Rosen, of “Operation Varsity Blues” fame, Dynamis represents individuals and companies in all manner of government-facing investigations, including matters before the DOJ, SEC, CFTC and various state regulators and agencies. In a precedent setting result, Eric recently obtained the dismissal of the Indictment for all seven defendants in the “Atlas Trading” pump-and-dump case in Houston, Texas.

Government and Internal Investigations

We are highly-skilled attorneys, committed to conducting thorough, detailed investigations that leave no stone unturned.

Business Disputes and Commercial Litigation

Whether on the plaintiff or defense side, Dynamis attorneys have achieved tremendous results litigating on behalf of individual and corporate clients in federal and state courts nationwide in matters ranging from antitrust to real estate disputes to breach of fiduciary duty claims.

Financial Services and Securities

We have broad experience in nearly every facet of financial services, litigating both government-facing disputes (SEC, DOJ, FINRA) as well as private securities fraud actions. Dynamis attorneys have also litigated on behalf of plaintiffs and defendants in individual case and class actions under the Securities Act, Exchange Act, and PSLRA.

Class Actions

Dynamis attorneys have prosecuted and defended class actions, class arbitrations, and mass arbitrations, and we know how to navigate the procedural and substantive advantages and disadvantages from the plaintiffs’ or defendants’ side.

Real Estate Litigation

Dynamis attorneys have litigated high-value partnership disputes involving commercial real estate, and we understand the need for strategic, cost-effective state and/or federal litigation, while exploring dynamic, beneficial options for resolution.

Fintech & Crypto Bankruptcy Litigation

Dynamis has wide-ranging experience navigating complex litigation in connection with large-scale Fintech/Crypto bankruptcies. When these businesses become insolvent—because of fraud, economic forces, regulatory issues, or otherwise—The Great Recovery represents executives, creditors, clawback defendants, or even debtors in addressing the legion of novel legal issues. We understand the nuances of a trustee’s duties to recover and distribute funds to creditors and to commence adversary proceedings against recipients of preferential or fraudulent transfers.

Crypto and Blockchain

Dynamis attorneys have years of experience litigating all forms of crypto disputes, whether as part of government investigations, criminal defense, internal investigations, class-actions, or business disputes. Dynamis attorneys are shaping precedent in this cutting-edge field, and are some of the most accomplished and called-upon attorneys in crypto-oriented litigation.

Whistleblower Lawsuits

Dynamis attorneys frequently represent whistleblowers who wish to report misconduct to the government and receive a share of the recovery. We represent whistleblowers nationwide in cases involving violations of the False Claims Act, securities laws, commodities and futures laws, and laws regulating the banking industry.

International Disputes Investigations and Enforcement (FCPA)

We have significant and diverse experience with international disputes, investigations and enforcement actions, including FCPA investigations led by the Department of Justice and the Securities and Exchange Commission.

Providing zealous, aggressive, and sophisticated advocacy when our clients need it the most.
